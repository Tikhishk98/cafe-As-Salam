Welcome

As Salam Cafe started the business from May 2018.

About Us

It start when a couple of friends studying culinary science who want to open a cafe with a western-themed food. As Salam Cafe was founded by Miss Kaka and her besties Miss Winwin and began operations in October 2018. Besides the aesthetics, patrons can look forward to appetising dishes with fusion flavours that are carefully curated to suit the local palate.
